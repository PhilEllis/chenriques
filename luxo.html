Luxo Apartment

  • Category: Holiday Let
  • Website: www.booking.com

Beautiful Two Bed Apartment By The Beach

Experience the perfect blend of sun, sand and relaxation in the golden coastline of Consolacao, Portugal. Our holiday apartment features spacious bedrooms, furnished living areas, and a fully equipped kitchen, allowing you to enjoy the comforts of home while reveling in a truly idyllic getaway.

Peniche is renowned for its unspoiled beauty and relaxed ambiance, offering a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Immerse yourself in the local culture, stroll along pristine beaches, or embark on a thrilling adventure exploring the nearby natural wonders. Whether you seek adventure or tranquility, Peniche has something for everyone.

Conveniently situated just a short distance from Peniche’s vibrant town center, our holiday apartment allows you to easily explore charming local shops, savor delicious cuisine at seaside restaurants, and immerse yourself in the rich history and culture of the region. Discover hidden gems, meet friendly locals, and create memories that will last a lifetime.

We can't wait to welcome you to Portugal!
