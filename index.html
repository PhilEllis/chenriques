About Me

About Me

My picture of success used to look a lot different than it does now. I kept striving for the seemingly unachievable until I took a moment, released myself from the version of me I had created to survive, and realised I already had it all: happiness, health, and community.

So, let me introduce myself and share with you my passion for building businesses with purpose!

A Purpose-Driven Journey

At the heart of everything I do are five core values that shape my life: Legacy, Honesty, Purpose, Balance, and Generosity. These values are not just words; they are the guiding principles that drive my decisions, actions, and interactions with others. Sprinkle in my unyielding tenacity, professional nagging and maybe just a few swear words and you have my book loving, sea dipping, spiritually aligned self perfectly summed up.

Balancing Motherhood and Entrepreneurship

My mum brought me up to be hardworking and equally I have found my journey into motherhood has been a transformative experience. It has taught me that there's more to life than just professional success. With creativity and determination, I've found a way to be present for my children while continuing to build successful businesses.

Embracing Diversity and Open-Mindedness

The diversity of my background has not only shaped my identity but also enabled me to embrace an open-minded approach to life and business, allowing me bring a unique perspective to my ventures.

Nurturing Businesses with Purpose

I find immense joy in nurturing businesses that align with my values and contribute positively to society. For me, it's not just about the bottom line; it's about leaving a lasting legacy that inspires and empowers others to create positive change.
