Crystal Chakra Candles

Being an avid lover of candles, I wanted to add some physical element to the healing work that I do so I came up with 7 different candles, one for each chakra. It’s intended to align and balance the body's energy centres.

By using candles that contain crystals associated with each chakra, the candle's flame and energy can positively influence the corresponding chakra. Each crystal is carefully chosen based on its colour and the energy it possesses.

When the candle is burned, the energy of the crystals is released and combined with the energy of the flame, creating a harmonious environment and helping to balance and cleanse the associated chakra. You can use these crystal chakra candles during meditation, energy healing sessions, or simply as a decorative element to enhance your beautiful living space.
