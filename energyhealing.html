What is QHEH

Quantum Holographic Echo Healing is a modality that involves energy healing techniques aimed at promoting physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being by working with the body's energy systems to facilitate healing and balance. It draws from concepts in quantum physics and holistic healing to address various aspects of a person's well-being.

Our subconscious mind is a reservoir of memories, experiences, thoughts, emotions, and beliefs acquired throughout our life journey, including those inherited from our ancestors through ancestral DNA. Even if we can't consciously recall a specific memory or event that gave rise to a limiting belief—like a sense of insecurity linked to your mother's early environment while you were an infant or even in the womb—our cells retain every emotional imprint. These emotional imprints are registered by the amygdala, the emotional memory hub of the brain, and communicated to the limbic system, which governs fear-driven responses.

Consequently, as adults, we're instinctively prompted to feel, think, and act based on these long-forgotten emotional imprints. These responses can restrict us, especially if they stem from past negative encounters.
