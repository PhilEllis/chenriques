What is QHEH

Quantum Holographic Echo Healing is a modality that involves energy healing techniques aimed at promoting physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being by working with the body's energy systems to facilitate healing and balance. It draws from concepts in quantum physics and holistic healing to address various aspects of a person's well-being.

Do you find yourself more drawn to Spirituality or Science? I don’t feel that it’s necessarily an either-or choice – I believe that the two can coexist harmoniously.

Our subconscious mind is a reservoir of memories, experiences, thoughts, emotions, and beliefs acquired throughout our life journey, including those inherited from our ancestors through ancestral DNA.

Even if we can't consciously recall a specific memory or event that gave rise to a limiting belief—like a sense of insecurity linked to your mother's early environment while you were an infant or even in the womb—our cells retain every emotional imprint. These emotional imprints are registered by the amygdala, the emotional memory hub of the brain, and communicated to the limbic system, which governs fear-driven responses.

Consequently, as adults, we're instinctively prompted to feel, think, and act based on these long-forgotten emotional imprints. These responses can restrict us, especially if they stem from past negative encounters. Transitioning from the impact of these imprints, people explore energy healing for transformative results:

  • Balance and Stress Relief: A release of emotional blockages and tension stored in your body can lead to a greater sense of emotional balance, reduced stress, and an overall improved sense of well-being.
  • Spiritual Growth and Self-Discovery: People often choose energy healing to foster personal growth, gain insights into their life's purpose, and deepen their connection to their spirituality.

Practitioners like me focus on identifying and releasing energy blockages, emotional imprints, and limiting beliefs stored in the subconscious mind. Through various methods like guided visualisation, intention setting, and energy manipulation, they aim to promote self-discovery, inner harmony, and holistic healing.
