Energy Healing

Embracing the role of a QHEH Practitioner, I've delved into comprehending the intricate workings of my mind, body, and spirit. This journey has transformed me significantly, fostering a deeper sense of fulfilment and wisdom. With this newfound understanding, I am eager to share the benefits of this practice with those around me, particularly those within the corporate realm.

Open Your Mind: Let me take you through my transformative journey and show you the power of QHEH Healing!

What Drew Me To QHEH?

Much like countless others, I found myself In a low period. Struggling to shake off the grip of persistent depression. Reminiscing about the profound emotional release I experienced after receiving Reiki I started to research and stumbled upon Quantum Holographic Echo Healing, a fresh modality that immediately captured my attention. At this point I had done all the right things I was meditating, getting outdoors, exercising and eating well but nothing seemed to work. I Recognised the potential of QHEH to pull me out of my funk and was instantly drawn to explore it further.

Embracing Lighter Emotions and Self-Rediscovery

After my initial QHEH session, an incredible transformation swept over me. Stepping out of that session, I was met with a rush of familiar emotions and a revived connection with my true self. Enthusiasm, motivation, and excitement surged back into my life. The profound impact was undeniable.

Left Craving Deeper Understanding

Intrigued and driven by my natural curiosity, I decided to delve deeper into this modality. Rather than merely seeking more sessions, I chose to enrol in the course. Understanding the mechanics behind QHEH became imperative; I desired the ability to heal myself whenever the need arose. During my training, it struck me that countless individuals could benefit from this form of treatment. This realisation sparked a journey of sharing, as I began training my loved ones and friends. Witnessing their positive transformations brought me an indescribable sense of fulfilment and satisfaction.

Unforeseen Reward: Discovering How Assisting Others Has Amplified My Personal Growth

Little did I anticipate the rewarding twist that awaited me on this journey. As I extended the healing benefits of QHEH to those around me, an unexpected synergy emerged. The act of helping others find their paths to healing, growth, and transformation became a profound source of nourishment for my own journey. Breaking free from limiting narratives and stagnant cycles wasn't just a gift I offered others – it became a reciprocal process that enriched my own relationship with life. The beauty of healing others lies in the parallel healing it ignites within me, creating a harmonious loop of mutual growth and empowerment that continues to inspire me each day.

"QHEH has became a precious gift that I want to share with you."


- Carina Henriques

What is it

What is QHEH

Quantum Holographic Echo Healing is a modality that involves energy healing techniques aimed at promoting physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being by working with the body's energy systems to facilitate healing and balance. It draws from concepts in quantum physics and holistic healing to address various aspects of a person's well-being.
